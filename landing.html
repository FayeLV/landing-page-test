FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why should I paint my brick home?

Painting your brick home enhances curb appeal, provides protection against weather elements, and helps prevent mold and mildew growth. A fresh coat of paint can transform the look of your home, making it more attractive and increasing its value.

How long does brick paint last?

With proper application and high-quality products like RomaBio Masonry Flat paint, brick paint can last 15-20 years. Our paint is chip- and peel-resistant, ensuring long-lasting coverage and minimal maintenance.

What is the best paint for brick surfaces?

RomaBio Masonry Flat paint is the best choice for brick surfaces. It’s a breathable, full-coverage matte paint designed specifically for masonry, offering a rich, textured look and exceptional durability. It’s also environmentally friendly, mold-resistant, and toxin-free.

Can I choose any color for my brick painting project?

Absolutely! We offer custom color-matching options to match your desired color scheme. Whether you want to match trims or soffits or create a unique look, our team can help you achieve the perfect color for your home.

Does brick painting require a lot of maintenance?

Brick painting with RomaBio Masonry Flat paint requires minimal maintenance. The paint is designed to be long-lasting and resistant to chipping, peeling, and fading, ensuring your home looks great for years with little upkeep.
